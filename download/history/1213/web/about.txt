# Our Mission

Our slogan this year is “Everyone can be a mathematician!” Being one of the best academic clubs at St. Joseph's College, we shall strive our best to:

- Share the joy of learning Mathematics
- Cultivate students' interest in Mathematics
- Provide students with a suitable learning environment
- Broaden the horizons of students in Mathematics
- Enhance a good mathematics atmosphere around SJC
- Raise students' mathematics ability by providing training programmes
- Train potential students to participate in Mathematics Olympiad Competition
